# Major Banks in the US

## Overview

The US banking sector is dominated by the "Big Four" — JPMorgan Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, and Citigroup — which together hold roughly $10 trillion in assets. Beyond these, major players include Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, U.S. Bancorp, Truist, and PNC Financial.
